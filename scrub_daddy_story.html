The Origin Of Scrub Daddy

Like many great inventions, Scrub Daddy was born out of necessity. In 2006, Aaron Krause, an entrepreneur and product designer, was working on buffing pads for the automotive industry. During the process, he developed a special foam material with Flex Texture® technology, which had the ability to change its firmness depending on the water temperature.

This innovative material was the precursor to what would later become Scrub Daddy. At first, the foam was just a side project for Krause. However, one day, while cleaning his patio furniture, Krause realized that the unique material could be a game-changer in the world of household cleaning products.
